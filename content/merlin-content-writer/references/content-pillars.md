# Content Pillars

## Distribution

| Pillar | Share | Focus |
|--------|-------|-------|
| Cases & Frameworks | 50% | Implementation breakdowns with numbers |
| Build-in-public | 30% | Agency process, experiments, real progress |
| Insights & Opinions | 15% | Author's take on trends with practical angle |
| Soft promo | 5% | CTA in useful content, not standalone |

---

## 1. Cases & Frameworks (50%)

Breakdowns of implementations with architecture and concrete numbers. Hormozi-style formulas.

**Topic examples:**
- "How lead qualification system saved SaaS startup 40 hours/month"
- "ROI formula for sales automation: if your SDR spends X hours..."
- "5 questions for auditing sales department automation"
- "When NOT to implement AI: checklist of 7 criteria"

---

## 2. Build-in-public (30%)

Agency creation process openly: what works, what doesn't, iterations.

**Topic examples:**
- "Week 4 of agency: first pilot, here's what broke"
- "Testing Claude vs GPT-4 for lead qualification — results"
- "Turned down a client. Here's why it was right"
- "Month 2: revenue, lessons, what's next"

---

## 3. Insights & Opinions (15%)

Author's perspective on news and trends — always with practical angle.

**Topic examples:**
- "OpenAI released X — here's where it's actually applicable"
- "Why 90% of AI projects fail (and how to be in the 10%)"
- "Breakdown: how [famous company] built their AI system"

---

## 4. Soft Promo (5%)

CTA embedded in useful content. Never standalone promo posts.

**Format:** End useful post with "If you want your case analyzed — write 'audit' in DM"

---

## Publication Parameters

| Parameter | Value |
|-----------|-------|
| Frequency | 3 posts/week |
| Length | 800-2000 characters (flexible) |
| Time | Weekdays 9-11 or 19-21 |
| Visuals | Architecture diagrams, screenshots, minimal stock |
