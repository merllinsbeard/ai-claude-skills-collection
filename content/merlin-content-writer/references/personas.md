# Target Personas

## Layer 1: Decision Makers (20% audience, 80% value)

### Founder Fedor
**Who:** SaaS founder, 30-42, company 10-50 people, $500K-5M ARR. Technically literate, skeptical of AI hype.

**Content that resonates:**
- ROI cases with concrete numbers from similar companies
- Frameworks and formulas (Hormozi-style)
- Build-in-public with real metrics

**Language:** unit economics, CAC payback, pipeline velocity, scale, efficiency, proof of concept

**Messaging angle:** "Systems that impact metrics, measurable ROI, scaling without proportional hiring"

---

### CMO Katya
**Who:** VP/Head of Marketing, 32-45, company 50-500 people. Under pressure from CEO/CFO to prove marketing ROI.

**Content that resonates:**
- Enterprise case studies with ROI data
- Industry benchmarks
- Integration-focused content (works with existing stack)

**Language:** attribution, pipeline contribution, marketing ROI, MarTech stack, board-ready

**Messaging angle:** "Provable ROI for CFO/Board, integration not replacement, quick wins for internal buy-in"

---
