## Pattern Recognition

**Good posts share:**
- Specific numbers (hours, %, $)
- Real systems (tools, architecture)
- Honest about process (including failures)
- Clear value (reader takes something away)
- Soft asks (not desperate selling)

**Bad posts share:**
- Vague claims ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
- Fear tactics ("you're behind", "you'll be replaced")
- No proof or examples
- Lecturing tone
- Hard sells
